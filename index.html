About Me

I am a South African born, North Carolina bred, LA transplant who finds learning and writing code one of the most exhilarating experiences I have encountered to date.

Over the last few years I have worked in varying industries. I have experience in administration, office management, property management and project management. Within those roles I seemed to always gravitate to the more technical aspects of my job duties. My fascination with making daily tasks more efficient by finding ways to automate them lead me to discover the use of Macros and VBA in excel. The ability to edit the code recorded in the macro is what sparked the interest in learning more about coding.

Solving efficiency issues with the use of automation, the ability to manipulate code to complete tasks and the possibility for infinite creativity though the use of code is what drives my passion to continue on this journey of learning.
